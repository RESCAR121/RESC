About

Five Nights at Freddy's (often called FNaF) is a horror game franchise created by Scott Cawthon. The first game was released in 2014 and puts players in the role of a nighttime security guard watching over a family entertainment restaurant. The main challenge is surviving against animatronic characters that become dangerous after dark, using limited power and security cameras to track their movements. What made FNaF especially popular was its atmosphere, jump scares, and mysterious story. Over the years, the series expanded into many games, books, and even the Five Nights at Freddy's film. Fans enjoy piecing together clues hidden throughout the games to understand the complex lore involving haunted animatronics, missing children, and the history of the fictional company behind the restaurants. The most famous animatronics include Freddy Fazbear, Bonnie, Chica, and Foxy, all of whom have become iconic characters in modern horror gaming.
